# Global master’s shortlist (Indian applicant)

**Profile (unchanged):** BBA (India), business/customer-success background, target **~12 months** of study, **post-study work with relatively clear rules**, **tuition + typical mandatory university fees ≈ CAD $30,000 or less** (living, visa, flights, blocked-account/GIC-style proof-of-funds **not** included).

**FX used for comparison (approx., mid-2026):** CAD $30,000 ≈ **US $22,000** · **€20,000** · **£17,500** · **AUD $33,000** · **NZD $37,000**.

**“Surety” scale used here**

| Tier | Meaning |
|------|--------|
| **A** | Open post-study work right for master’s graduates; **no job offer** required upfront; duration and rules are statutory and well documented. |
| **B** | Dedicated **job-seeker** residence (fixed months); can work while searching; PR/long-term stay depends on finding qualifying employment. |
| **C** | Work right exists but **short**, employer-linked, lottery-based, or **study length** rules make a 1-year plan risky. |

## Deeper notes by shortlisted country

### 1. Canada (reference)

- **Pathway:** Master’s at PGWP-eligible **public** DLI → **up to 3-year PGWP** (IRCC; master’s rule since 2024).
- **Budget:** Your verified CSV shows **~56** programs under/borderline **CAD $30k**; premium MBAs (e.g. Lakehead ~**$45k**, UNB SJ MBA ~**$37k**) are explicitly out.
- **Fit for you:** Laurier MSc Management, SMU Applied Economics, UNB MA Economics, MUN course-based master’s (higher tuition than old “$8k” myths), etc.

### 2. Ireland

- **Pathway:** [Third Level Graduate Programme](https://www.irishimmigration.ie/my-situation-has-changed-since-i-arrived-in-ireland/third-level-graduate-programme/) — Level **9** master’s → **24 months** max (12 + 12 with renewal).
- **Fees:** Taught master’s commonly **€10k–€25k**; business/MBA toward top of range.
- **Surety:** High for master’s; must apply within **6 months** of results, maintain insurance, **€300** IRP fee.
- **CSV focus:** 1-year Level 9 programmes ≤ ~**€20k** intl tuition + mandatory charges.

### 3. United Kingdom

- **Pathway:** [Graduate Route](https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa) — **2 years** (master’s) if application timing meets current rules; **18 months** from **1 Jan 2027** for new applicants.
- **Fees:** London and Russell Group often **>£25k**; **regional/post-92** unis can be **£12k–£15k** (e.g. York St John, Leeds Trinity bands, Chester ~£15k) — within CAD $30k.
- **Surety:** High (open work); **no direct PR**—Skilled Worker route later.
- **CSV focus:** 1-year taught MSc/MBA in business/economics/management at **≤£17.5k** intl fee.

### 4. Germany

- **Pathway:** **18-month** residence to seek employment after German degree ([Make it in Germany](https://www.make-it-in-germany.com/) / local Ausländerbehörde).
- **Fees:** Nominal at most public universities; budget for **blocked account** (~**€11,904+/year** living proof) separately.
- **Caveats:** **2-year** MSc common; **non-consecutive** / MBA may charge tuition; **Baden-Württemberg** and **TUM** charge non-EU fees; **VPD/ANABIN** for Indian degrees.
- **CSV focus:** English-taught **MSc Management / Business / Economics** at **public** unis with **total mandatory costs ≤ €20k** for realistic program length (often 4 semesters).

### 5. France

- **Pathway:** Job-search residence for graduates (APS-style); confirm current duration on official immigration sources when building CSV.
- **Fees:** Differentiated master’s **€3,941/year** (2025–26); **exemption caps** for non-EU students tightening (**30%** cap 2026–27)—always confirm per university.
- **CSV focus:** English or French **M1/M2** and **1-year MS** programmes under **€20k total tuition**; flag grandes écoles separately (often over budget).

### 6. Portugal

- **Pathway:** [Article 122](https://liveinpt.com/guides/article-122-portugal-stay-after-student-visa/) — **1 year** after master’s completed in Portugal; **AIMA** process (not the old generic job-seeker visa).
- **Fees:** Among the lowest in Western Europe for public institutions.
- **Caveats:** Admin heavy; many programmes **2 years**; proof of subsistence required.

### 7. Poland

- **Pathway:** **9-month** graduate temporary residence + evolving **work-permit exemption** only for **listed** full-time university graduates ([gov.pl](https://www.gov.pl/web/udsc-en/permit-for-temporary-residence--graduate)).
- **Fees:** Low by EU standards.
- **Caveats:** **2-year** master’s typical; English business programmes exist but fewer than UK/Ireland; labour market rules changed **July/Dec 2025**.

### 8. Italy

- **Pathway:** **12-month** job-seeker permesso for graduates.
- **Fees:** Public universities often **€1k–€4k/year**; elite privates (Bocconi, LUISS) usually **over** CAD $30k.
- **Caveats:** **2-year** master’s standard; English supply growing but course list smaller than UK.

### 9. New Zealand

- **Pathway:** [Post Study Work Visa](https://www.immigration.govt.nz/visas/post-study-work-visa/) — **up to 3 years** if master’s completed with **≥30 weeks** full-time study in NZ.
- **Fees:** Business master’s often **above** CAD $30k equivalent—CSV should **strictly filter** or tag “over budget”.
- **Surety:** High **if** you meet study-length rules.

## Cross-cutting admission notes (India + BBA)

- **English tests:** IELTS/TOEFL/PTE thresholds vary; several countries now require **higher** scores for post-study visas (e.g. Australia 485, Canada PGWP **CLB 7** for some streams)—we will tag these in each CSV `Notes` column.
- **Business master’s types:** Prefer **MSc Management / Applied Economics / International Business** over research PhD-track MSc unless you want academia.
- **“1 year” vs work visa:** Always pair **program length on offer letter** with **that country’s minimum study period** for post-study work (Australia is the main trap).
