**Last updated:** 2026-07-23  
**FX used:** 1 EUR = 1.47 CAD (see `research/FX-RATES.md`)

## Immigration (post-study)

- **Pathway:** Residence permit to seek skilled employment after a German degree (AufenthG §20).
- **Duration:** Up to **18 months**; unrestricted work while searching.
- **Official source:** [Make it in Germany — studying (prospects after graduation)](https://www.make-it-in-germany.com/en/visa-residence/types/studying)

## Fee methodology

| Case | Assumption (non-EU, 4 semesters unless noted) |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Most public Länder | €0 tuition; **~€320/sem** Semesterbeitrag |
| **Baden-Württemberg** | **€1,500/sem** non-EU tuition + **~€194/sem** services |
| **Bavaria (LMU/Passau etc.)** | €0 tuition; **~€85/sem** contribution |
| **Berlin** | €0 tuition; **~€315/sem** |
| **TUM** | **€4,000–€6,000/sem** non-EU master's tuition + contribution |

**Budget (Tier B Germany):** Yes ≤ CAD 30,000; Borderline 30,001–32,000; > CAD 32,000 → `No`.

## Indian applicant notes

- **APS** (India/China/Vietnam) for visa; many unis require **VPD** via uni-assist.
- Check **ANABIN** (H+ institution) and quantitative ECTS requirements.
