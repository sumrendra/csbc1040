## Russell Group ≤ £22k (2026/27 campus MSc/MBA business)

No Russell Group **on-campus** business/management MSc/MBA was verified at **≤ £22,000** international published tuition for 2026/27. Typical Russell business MSc intl fees are **£25k–£38k+** (e.g. Exeter MSc Management £31,000; Sheffield MSc Management ~£32,905). **Premium_OK** rows include strong post-92 options (MMU, Northumbria) instead.

## Gaps / caveats

- Some institutions publish fees on central schedules; exact title should be confirmed on the **course offer**.
- York St John / Hull / Lincoln: fee displayed via course UI or cross-page — re-check at CAS stage.
- **Excluded:** 2-year MA Social Work; 2-year MSc with mandatory placement unless separate 1-year route exists.
- Online-only programmes (e.g. Liverpool online MSc Management £17,947) excluded from campus Graduate Route shortlist unless physically studying in UK.
