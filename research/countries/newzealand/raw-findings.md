## PSWV — 30-week rule (master's → up to 3 years)

**Official sources**

- Post Study Work Visa: https://www.immigration.govt.nz/visas/post-study-work-visa/
- Duration / 3 years for master's: https://www.immigration.govt.nz/work/requirements-for-work-visas/how-long-you-can-work-on-work-visas/how-long-you-can-stay-on-a-post-study-work-visa/
- PGDip → short master's pathway: https://www.immigration.govt.nz/about-us/news-centre/changes-to-the-post-study-work-visa-pswv/

**Rules (summary for planning)**

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Qualification | NZQCF level 7+ degree (includes level 9 master's). |
| Location | Studied **full-time in New Zealand**. |
| Minimum duration | **At least 30 weeks** on the qualification you rely on for the visa. |
| Master's benefit | Master's or doctoral completed with ≥30 weeks in NZ → PSWV **up to 3 years**, open work (any employer, any role) for degree level 7+. |
| Short master's | If you complete a **PGDip (or other eligible qual) for ≥30 weeks** and **immediately** enrol in a higher master's studied **&lt;30 weeks**, you may apply for PSWV **based on the first qualification** within **12 months** after that student visa ends — **not** a 3-year visa on the master's alone. |
| Offer of place | INZ may use your offer of place to assess study length; failing courses / extending beyond the offer does not extend PSWV length. |
| Application window | Generally within **3 months** of student visa expiry (special rules for consecutive qualifications — see INZ page). |

**Implications for Indian BBA → coursework master's**

- Standard **180-point** programmes (12–18 months, two–three semesters) normally exceed 30 weeks on the **master's enrolment** → eligible for **3-year PSWV** if all other criteria met.
- **120-point** one-year taught or thesis master's (~12 months) still typically ≥30 weeks calendar study in NZ; confirm **start/end dates on offer of place**.
- Do **not** plan on PGDip (30 weeks) + stacked 120-point master's in &lt;30 weeks unless you accept PSWV tied to the diploma (shorter work rights, job relevance rules differ for sub-degree quals).
- **Online / offshore study** does not count toward the 30 weeks in NZ.

## Budget reality (business coursework master's)

At 2026 published international rates, **almost all 180-point coursework business master's exceed CAD 38k** total tuition. None clearly sit in `Yes` (≤CAD 30k) without major scholarships.

**Nearest to brief ceiling (NZD 45k / CAD ~36.9k):**

- **Otago MCom (120pt)** ~NZD 40,740 tuition — **Borderline** CAD ~33.4k (research thesis; not general coursework conversion).
- **Waikato / Victoria 120-point annual commerce bands** (~NZD 38–41k) apply to **one year of study**, not typical 180pt master's total.

**Premium_OK window (NZD 45k):** No full 180pt business master's identified at or below NZD 45k programme tuition on official 2026 tables; Lincoln taught programmes ~NZD 57k+.
