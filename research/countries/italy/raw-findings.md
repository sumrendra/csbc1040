## Immigration (post-study)

- **Pathway:** *Permesso di soggiorno per ricerca lavoro o imprenditorialità degli studenti* — Art. **39-bis.1** of D.Lgs. 286/1998 (TUI), implementing EU Directive 2016/801 (D.Lgs. 71/2018).
- **Duration:** **9–12 months** (planning uses **12 months**); seek employment or start a business aligned with studies completed in Italy.
- **Prerequisites:** Degree awarded in Italy; register **D.I.D.** (dichiarazione di immediata disponibilità) at **Centro per l’Impiego**; apply via **postal kit code 30** to the Questura.
- **Official sources:**
  - [Integrazione migranti — job-search permesso for graduates](https://www.integrazionemigranti.gov.it/it-it/Ricerca-news/Dettaglio-news/id/3301)
  - [Polizia di Stato — Vademecum titoli di soggiorno (Studente/Ricerca Lavoro)](https://questure.poliziadistato.it/statics/56/vademecum-titoli-di-soggiorno.pdf?lang=it)

## Fee methodology

| Case | Assumption (non-EU, 2-year Laurea Magistrale) |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **Public universities (state)** | Contribution/tuition bands typically **€900–€4,000/year** (ISEE/contribution classes; regional maxima). |
| **Planning total (JSON)** | **€8,000** = 2 × €4,000/yr ceiling → **CAD 11,760** at 1.47. |
| **Excluded** | **Bocconi**, **LUISS**, and other private fee-based schools (per BRIEF), unless separately tagged Borderline. |

**Budget (Tier B Italy):** `Yes` ≤ CAD 30,000; `Borderline` 30,001–32,000; `No` > CAD 32,000 for tuition + mandatory university fees. All listed public LM programmes fall in **`Yes`** under the planning ceiling.

## Indian applicant notes

- **Degree recognition:** CIMEA / ENIC-NARIC statements often required for admission and visa.
- **Pre-enrollment:** [Universitaly](https://www.universitaly.it/) portal for many institutions; verify each programme call.
- **Visa:** Long-stay study visa (D) before travel; **permesso per motivi di studio** within 8 days of arrival.
- **English:** B2 (CEFR) typical for English-taught LM; BBA in English may waive certification.
- **Curricular fit:** LM-56/LM-77 programmes commonly require 48–60 ECTS in economics, statistics, mathematics, and/or business SSDs.
